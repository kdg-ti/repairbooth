Welcome, have a repair

A Repair Cafe is virtual place where people can have broken things made by craftsmen. Every repair can be assessed by the customer. Craftsmen are assessed according to the quality of their repairs. As a customer of the Repair Cafe, you can thus always know who you entrust your things to.

You can register as a customer and/or craftsman at the Repair Cafe. Customers can have their belongings repaired by the best craftsman at the best price and professionals can look for repairs to help them earn a penny.
